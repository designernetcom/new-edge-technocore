About Us

New Edge Technocore

Pioneering Green Energy & Turnkey EPC Solutions Globally

The New Edge Technocore, is a global EPC and turnkey project management company head quartered in Pune, Maharashtra. We offer single point responsibility solutions and services for engineering and construction of industrial plants, from concept to commissioning. New Edge has been efficaciously providing EPC services across a wide spectrum of sectors such as Power Plant, Distilleries, Sugar Industry, Water & Waste Management Solutions, Oil & Gas, Chemical & Fertilizer Industry, Pharmaceuticals, Paper & Pulp and Textile Industry. We deliver projects with best in class quality, on-time delivery and completion within budget. The company is also a green field solution provider; providing technology, technique layout and engineering, supply, construction, commissioning and kind of complete solutions to various core industry sectors.

Our Approach

Understanding Customer Requirement

We get involved in understanding the complete requirements, processes & associated variations along with end use application prior to arriving at the optimum solution. Each requirement is properly understood and studied by the front end function to ensure committed deliverance.

Technologies & System Integration

We keep abreast with the latest global and in-house developed products and technologies. It’s core experienced backend function ensures correct implementation and integration of technologies to provide state of the art systems.

Design & Proposal Engineering

We have a fully developed in-house design & proposal engineering function equipped with latest automated total engineering CAD software, field data of various influents and latest technologies to create & provide reliable, low operating cost, robust, optimized and value added solutions.

Manufacturing & Site Installation

We follow a focused and stringent Quality Assurance Plan in-house & with its long standing vendors with strong teamwork at site to provide on-time deliverance.

After Sales Support & Operations

We have a dedicated well-trained service team to erect, commission, provide on-site & board room training and complete backup support with planned maintenance schedules, thus ensuring minimum downtime. Further, it undertakes comprehensive operation and maintenance of the supplied systems on committed & actual performance parameters.

Research & Development
