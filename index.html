IN FOCUS

Regenerative Thermal
Oxidizer

Adwest, EIS, and WK brands deliver cutting-edge air pollution control solutions, specializing in Regenerative Thermal Oxidizers (RTOs) to meet the environmental needs of industries worldwide. RTOs effectively reduce volatile organic compounds (VOCs), hazardous air pollutants (HAPs), and odors by oxidizing them at high temperatures into harmless byproducts like carbon dioxide and water.

Hee-duall Bio-pro Biofiltration
Landing Page

Hee-Duall Bio-Pro biofiltration systems are designed to protect the environment and people from harmful pollutants like corrosive gases, hazardous air pollutants, and odors. The system incorporates green technology that reduces NOx and CO2 emissions, eliminates the use of fossil fuels, and optimizes energy and water efficiency. They offer a low total cost of ownership by providing cost-effective solutions with long equipment life, efficiency, and preventive maintenance programs. Carbon Capture, Utilization,
and Storage (CCUS)

Carbon Capture, Utilization, and Storage (CCUS) stands at the forefront of global efforts to combat the climate crisis. By capturing CO₂ emissions from industrial processes and either utilizing them in practical applications or storing them securely underground, CCUS technologies offer a scalable solution to reduce greenhouse gas emissions drastically.

Eliminate Corrosion in
Marine Environments

Peerless composite louvers are designed to combat corrosion in marine environments, offering a durable and cost-effective alternative to traditional steel louvers. Made from fiber-reinforced plastic (FRP) composite materials, these louvers eliminate corrosion issues, reduce maintenance needs, and provide long-term savings. They are lightweight, UV-resistant, and thermally stable, making them ideal for harsh maritime conditions. Additionally, their advanced design incorporates radar-absorbing stealth technology for military applications.
